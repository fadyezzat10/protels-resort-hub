"PROTELS Crystal Beach Resort located in Marsa Alam, Red Sea, Egypt. A 4-star resort with 269 rooms and suites, all of them overlooking the magnificent Southern Red Sea (Natural Protected Area). The resort is built in an authentic Nubian & Arabic design. PROTELS Crystal Beach Resort is a preserved area in Marsa Alam, close to all the most beautiful dive sites with untouched offshore reefs. For the sea explorer, we have one of a kind jetty that will take you to the reef drop off where you will experience the best snorkeling and diving in the area. The beach at PROTELS Crystal Beach Resort compares to none other in the area with its exclusive private beach, all sandy 350-meter coastline making it perfect for beach chair sun soaking and safe for small children to play in the shallow water. Long walks and running on the beach area that extends up to two kilometers is another favorite for our guests. The Resort is all-inclusive property offering full board accommodation. On the beach overlooking the Red Sea in Marsa Alam, the Crystal Beach Resort features a private beach. The rooms are set around the garden and are designed in a Nubian style with domed ceilings.
