[Quiet hotel on the sea with European atmosphere: clean, delicious, but animation limps and swimming only from the pier](https://www.tripadvisor.com/ShowUserReviews-g311425-d33402481-r1024671160-Protels_Beach_Club_Spa-Marsa_Alam_Red_Sea_and_Sinai.html)

The hotel is located by the sea. Very pleasant territory and interesting architecture of buildings. The main guests are Czechs and Poles. There are Moroccans. Russians and Ukrainians - about 1% of all holidaymakers. Animators speak English, Polish and Czech.

Of the hotel's benefits:

• Clean, manicured area.

• Big beach with bar, volleyball nets, football field.

• Clear sea, your reef.

• Normal nutrition, you can always find something to your taste.

o For breakfast: pancakes, omelets, dry breakfasts, desserts, fresh vegetables, soft and semi-hard cheeses, sausage, sausages.

o For lunch: meat, poultry (the grill is delicious), fish, pizza, various side dishes, vegetables. Desserts are delicious. Fruits include watermelons, dates, pears, apples, melons, grapes, sometimes mangoes and plums.

• Rooms are clean, clean every day. But the towels come in really battered, the bathroom plumbing, too, especially the showers. There's no curtain, the water runs down to the floor and there's nothing to flush it down - just a foot towel.

o The TV wasn't working in our room, the air-conditioning remote was without a lid, and the batteries were falling out all the time.

• When applying to the reception, any problem was solved very quickly. Special thanks to Gaber Reyad: upon arrival he gave his working WhatsApp (there was no phone in the room - I don't know, so everywhere or selectively). He had to be approached about emerging issues, and even on his weekends he dealt with problems quickly and professionally.

Cons:

• You can only swim from the pier by the reef. The pier is open from 9 a.m. to 6 p.m., which is inconvenient for those who get up early. At 7–8 a.m. it is already very hot, and swimming from the shore is impossible – ankle-length water or slightly higher all the way to the reef (depending on the tides).

• Boring. Swimming in the pool and the sea all day is not interesting, you want activities. We love sea excursions, and there are only two from Mars Alam to Mars Mubarak Reserve and Dolphin House. Therefore, the animation team is a key moment in Mars-Alam hotels.

o This hotel animators are pleasant, have interesting shows, BUT there are shortcomings:

 There's no timetable board. You can only know what time volleyball or water polo is if you ask the animators.

 The plan for the day is announced only to participants of morning aquaerobics.

 Teams for games animators assemble themselves, approaching the campers. If you are not approached, you will not learn about the game and will not participate.

 They promised water polo every day at 3:00 p.m., but in 10 days the game was only once. As a result, the campers organized the games themselves.

• There is a strong smell of manure on the property periodically. It's not clear where - perhaps fertilizing lawns.

• Very stuffy in the restaurant and lobby. Air conditioners above 30°C can't handle. Especially hard at lunch: after 7-10 minutes, sweat runs down your back, and you want to go to the pool or room faster.

o The staff is polite, a special thanks to the head of the restaurant service Abdel Mohsen El Sayed and his colleague (I didn't remember the name) - watching the work, quickly setting the tables.

• Wi-Fi doesn't work almost anywhere. In the lobby, he barely catches and not always.

• Sometimes the power is cut for 10–15 minutes. There was no light once for an hour and a half.
