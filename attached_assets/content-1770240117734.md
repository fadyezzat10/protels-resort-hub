[Protels - don't search for other hotels :)](https://www.tripadvisor.com/ShowUserReviews-g482884-d34015525-r1048277826-Protels_La_Plage-Zanzibar_Island_Zanzibar_Archipelago.html)

Location: near to Paje and the Jozani forest. ~1 hour trip by car from/to the airport, good accessibility (road is OK on the whole ride to the hotel).

Atmosphere: LOVELY. It was really like a family, with the whole staff, including the managers, animators, waiters, everybody in the hotel.

Our room was comfortable, on the 1st floor with balcony, view to the pools and side-view to the ocean. The bed was king-sized and comfortable.

The room was always clean. We didn't ask but they cleaned our room almost every day. :)

We ordered all-inclusive service. The food was quite good mainly with continental dishes (chicken, pasta, pizza, daily soups, sometimes seafood - we would prefer even more :), cookies etc.).

We definitely got great value for our money!

Noise level is also good, nothing disturbed us during the night, we could properly rest.

We had our birthdays during our stay and we got surprised with a dinner on the beach with fantastic decoration. We loved it! We also got dedicated birthday cakes, they were very delicious.

There were some programme options within the hotel every day: aqua gym, darts, botcha (like petanque), beach-volley, karaoke-night, movie-night etc. They always tried to add something special to every day!

If you want to organise any programme outside the hotel, they are also can help you with almost everything.

All in all we are FULLY SATISFIED and would recommend this place! :)

Thanks a lot for the fantastic hospitality to all members of staff, especially to Faustine and Shady! :)
