Terrible Experience from Start to Finish – Don’t Waste Your Time or Money

Our stay at Protels Crystal Beach, Marsa Alam was a complete disappointment from the very moment we arrived.

After almost a 6-hour drive from Luxor, we arrived exhausted and expected at least a warm welcome. Instead, no one offered to help with our luggage, and the reception staff were completely disorganized, rude, and unprofessional. They took forever to give us our room keys, didn’t explain anything about the hotel facilities, meal times, or activities, and acted as if guests were a burden.

Things only got worse from there. The staff barely understand basic English and show zero interest in helping. Their attitude is arrogant and careless, and they clearly lack any training in hospitality.

The food was disgusting — both my wife and I got sick after eating at the buffet. Even the salads smelled spoiled, like they were kept out for days. The eggs at breakfast were full of eggshells, and when I politely pointed it out to the chef while he was cracking eggs for omelets, he just ignored me completely. It’s clear that hygiene and food safety are not priorities at this hotel.

The diving center, which was one of the main reasons we chose this resort, turned out to be a total scam. They overcharge for terrible trips with old, unsafe equipment and no proper food or service. The staff there are equally careless and unprofessional.

The pools are dirty, full of hair and floating plastic cups. There are not enough sunbeds at either the beach or the pool, and people fight over spots every morning. The chef at the beach restaurant was extremely rude and treated guests differently based on nationality — it felt outright discriminatory.

The rooms are old and not cleaned properly. Towels are not replaced daily, the air conditioning barely works, and everything feels neglected.

Overall, this place is chaotic, dirty, and mismanaged. The photos online are misleading — nothing matches reality. If you care about good service, hygiene, or even basic comfort, avoid Protels Crystal Beach. Marsa Alam has far better options; this hotel should not even be rated as a resort.
